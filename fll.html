HVFLL Tournament Schedule:

The tournament structure is ten qualifying events followed by a championship series starting with two semifinal events to select the teams that advance to the final event. Top scoring senior teams from each qualifying tournament will be invited to attend a semi-final. The top 6 teams from each semi-final will be invited to the final championship tournament at Dutchess Community College on Feb. 24th, 2018.
