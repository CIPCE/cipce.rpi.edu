The Rensselaer Science Ambassadors are an assembly of highly motivated undergraduate students focused on inspiring a younger audience to explore the role of science in society.

Schools

If you are an educator or representative of a school or district please download our SA Handout for Schools in order to learn about costs and how to register for a Science Ambassador visit.

Download our SA Outreach Request Form to schedule a visit. Once downloaded you may complete the pdf fields and return via email or send by mail.

Companies

Sponsor a Group of SA's

You can sponsor a group of Science Ambassadors to represent your company during outreach programs.

Your company has the option of identifying the criteria for our Science Ambassadors (SA) - such as science major, academic standings, and career focus.

Once selected your Science Ambassadors will research and develop a presentation and hands-on activity to take into school classrooms. The activities will be branded with your company’s logo. We will also promote your company on the SA website.

Sponsor a School Outreach Visit

We conduct a number of outreach visits to elementary, middle, and high schools within the Northeast.

Through a one-time gift, your company can sponsor one of these visits. Funds will support the expenses of equipment, travel, and support for a group of ten to twelve Ambassadors to visit a school and inspire the next generation of scientists.

We impact 250+ students during each visit. Your company is mentioned throughout the visit and is designated as a Sponsor of the day on the SA website.


If you are a company or industry and want to learn more about the various levels of sponsorship please download our
SA Sponsors Form.

While Science Ambassadors share their passion for science and STEM careers with a younger audience, they are also pursuing career pathways. Our SA program can help promote your organization while serving as an excellent recruitment tool. Sponsors support SA’s with internships, co-ops, site visits and career networking opportunities with employees.
