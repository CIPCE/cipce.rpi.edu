Outreach

CIPCE partners with school districts, neighborhood youth organizations, schools of education and teachers throughout the Capital Region to place RPI student mentors into after school enrichment activities for students ages 8-14. Programs include Animation & Gaming, LEGO® Robotics, FIRST LEGO League® (FLL) clubs and other technology initiatives.

Afterschool Clubs and Organizations

  • Brittonkill Central School District
  • Cohoes Middle School
  • City School District of Albany
  • Girls Inc. Albany
  • Trinity Alliance

Outreach Weekends

Thanks to outreach efforts sponsored by various departments on RPI campus, CIPCE mentors participate in a variety of spring outreach weekends designed to inspire visiting elementary, middle School and high school students to consider STEM fields.

  • Black Family Technology Awareness Day
  • Exploring Engineering Day
  • Design Your Future Day
  • STEP & Harlem Academy program as part of the Dean of Students Office Pre-College Initiatives

Engineering Academies

Our Summer Engineering Academies, both on and off campus, include programs such as Animation & Gaming with Scratch, Arduino, JrLEGO® Robotics, LEGO® NXT, Advanced LEGO® NXT and VEX Robotics. Participants design, plan, construct and reconstruct in a project-based, student-centered learning environment that fosters inquiry, innovation and critical thinking.

Visit Summer.rpi.edu and click on Summer Enrichment Programs.
